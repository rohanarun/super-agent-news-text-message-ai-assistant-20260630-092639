How a text message becomes a completed task

1. You text

“Reschedule my ride for 8am” or “Run the marketing play.” No app switching.

2. Super operates a computer

Super opens browsers or apps in the cloud and performs the exact clicks and typing required.

3. Cache reuse

The successful steps are saved in a computer-use cache so repeats are faster and cheaper.

4. You confirm

For sensitive actions, Super asks for confirmation before finishing.
